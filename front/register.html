Best Password Practices 🔐

  • Password must be at least 8 characters for strong security.
  • Include at least one uppercase letter (A-Z).
  • Include at least one number (0-9).
  • Include at least one special character (e.g., #, $, %, @, !, &, *).
  • Avoid using forbidden characters: ( ) " :
  • Use a unique email address for each registration.
  • Passwords are checked for strength and suggestions are provided as you type.
